A Belated Anniversary

your special day,
and I feel so sorry for being late. But the love you both share
is timeless,
and my wish for your happiness
and togetherness
comes straight from the heart,
even if it’s a couple of days late.

two of the most
beautiful souls I know.

strength, laughter, and the kind
of connection that inspires
everyone around you.
Watching the bond you both share

true love looks like.


May your journey together
continue to be filled with joy,
cherished memories, and endless love.
Here’s to celebrating you both,
not just on your anniversary but
every single day.



Sending love, blessings, and lots of hugs your way! ❤️
